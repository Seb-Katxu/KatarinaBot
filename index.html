KATARINA BOT

A simple Discord bot created by Katxu.

Katarina Bot commands


• kat!help : Show every available commands

• kat!mastery [ign] : show your 3 first champs with their mastery level

• kat!opgg [ign] : show your opgg profile

• kat!osuProfile [ign] : show your osu! profile

• kat!avatar : gives you your profile picture

• and many more to come
